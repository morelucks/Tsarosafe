# TsaroSafe

> A decentralized, blockchain-powered platform that transforms traditional community savings and lending systems into a modern, efficient, and secure solution.

## Overview

TsaroSafe is a decentralized, blockchain-powered platform that transforms traditional community savings and lending systems, such as Rotating Savings and Credit Associations (ROSCA), into a modern, efficient, and secure solution. By leveraging blockchain technology, TsaroSafe empowers individuals and communities to pool resources, save, earn interest, and access loans with unparalleled transparency, security, and automation.

Through smart contracts, TsaroSafe automates transactions and eliminates the need for intermediaries, ensuring that contributions, loans, and interest payouts are handled transparently, efficiently, and trustlessly. Whether saving for personal goals, investing with others, or borrowing funds, TsaroSafe offers a seamless and secure financial ecosystem.

## Mission

TsaroSafe's mission is to democratize access to secure, transparent, and interest-generating financial systems by transforming traditional community savings and lending models using blockchain technology. We empower individuals and communities, especially those underserved by traditional finance, to pool resources, earn interest, and access loans through automated, trustless smart contracts.

## Problem Statement

Traditional savings and lending systems face several critical issues:

- **Lack of Transparency**: Participants often lack insight into how funds and interest are managed
- **Trust Issues**: Risks of mismanagement or fraud by intermediaries or group leaders
- **Manual Processes**: Labor-intensive and error-prone tracking, fund distribution, and interest allocation methods
- **Limited Security**: Vulnerability to theft or loss due to inadequate protection systems
- **No Interest on Savings**: Traditional systems rarely provide meaningful returns for savers

## Solution

TsaroSafe tackles these challenges by combining blockchain technology and smart contracts to deliver:

- **Automated Transactions**: Contributions, withdrawals, loans, and interest payouts are seamlessly processed without manual intervention
- **Enhanced Transparency**: A public ledger records all transactions and interest distributions, visible to group members for full accountability
- **Improved Security**: Blockchain's cryptographic safeguards ensure the safety and immutability of funds
- **Interest Accrual**: Savers earn interest on their contributions from borrower repayments or group investments
- **Eliminated Trust Barriers**: Smart contracts enforce rules objectively, eliminating reliance on third parties

## Location of Impact

**Global** - TsaroSafe is designed to serve communities worldwide, with a focus on financial inclusion in emerging markets.

## Business Model

### 1. Value Proposition

- Provide a transparent, automated, and secure platform for group savings (e.g., ROSCAs)
- Enable interest-earning savings and community-driven lending without intermediaries
- Increase financial inclusion through decentralized technology

### 2. Customer Segments

- Low- and middle-income individuals in emerging markets seeking savings or microloans
- Informal savings groups (e.g., ROSCAs, tontines, cooperatives)
- NGOs, cooperatives, and microfinance institutions needing digital tools for group finance
- DeFi-savvy users seeking pooled savings opportunities with stable returns

### 3. Revenue Streams

- **Transaction Fees**: Small fee on interest earned, loans disbursed, or contributions processed (e.g., 0.5%–1%)
- **Premium Features / SaaS Tools**: Custom group management, analytics, white-label access for NGOs/MFIs
- **API Access or White-label Licensing**: Organizations pay to integrate or deploy TsaroSafe infrastructure

### 4. Key Activities

- Smart contract development and audits
- Platform maintenance and UI/UX improvements
- Community and partner onboarding
- Compliance with local financial and crypto regulations
- Education and user training (especially in underserved communities)

### 5. Key Resources

- Blockchain developers
- Smart contracts & DeFi infrastructure
- Community & education teams
- Strategic partnerships (NGOs, cooperatives, microfinance bodies)

### 6. Key Partners

- Blockchain networks (Celo for low fees)
- NGOs and community finance organizations
- Microfinance institutions (MFIs)
- Regulatory advisors
- Wallet providers and onboarding platforms (for easy KYC and fiat ramps)

### 7. Channels

- Mobile/Web app (primary platform)
- Community leaders as ambassadors
- NGO/MFI partnerships
- Social media and local marketing
- DeFi ecosystem integrations

### 8. Customer Relationships

- Trustless automation via smart contracts
- In-app support for onboarding and group formation
- Community-driven governance (if DAO)
- Education resources (videos, tutorials, local language content)

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- Node.js 18+ (for frontend)
- Foundry (for smart contracts)
- Git

### Installation

#### Smart Contracts

```bash
cd contracts
forge install
forge build
forge test
```

#### Frontend

```bash
cd frontend
npm install
npm run dev
```

## Technology Stack

- **Smart Contracts**: Solidity, Foundry
- **Frontend**: Next.js, TypeScript, Thirdweb
- **Blockchain**: Celo Network
